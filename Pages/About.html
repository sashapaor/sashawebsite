About Me

Sagitta Raine

Hello, I'm Raine

I'm an artist with a natural gift for drawing, bringing ideas to life with every stroke of my pencil. My love for art shows in the way I carefully sketch details, whether it's a simple portrait or a vivid landscape.

Drawing isn't just a hobby for me—it's my passion, a way to express my emotions and share my creativity with the world. Each piece I create reflects my talent, dedication, and the joy I find in my craft.

Through my work, I explore the intricacies of memory and identity, delving into the nuances of personal experiences and emotions that shape who we are.
